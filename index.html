Whats special about us?

Heartfelt is a software combo that enables to predict a coronary thrombosis and also identify the possible causes of it. This is based on a multi-dimensional study of coronary statistics and human behaviour to come to a possible state of conclusion.

Coronary Thrombosis is a fatal incident and gives a very less time to an individual to survive once it occurs. Hence, it is a better practice to predict possible chances of occurance. Therefore, there are several features that Heartfelt has to ensure a better chance of accurate prediction and safer well-being.

  • Multi-paramter Thrombosis Analysis
  • Medical support on click / call
  • Auto-diet planner to prevent junk eating.
  • Sentiment Analysis and keyword extraction for better problem understanding.
  • Yoga practioning webapp for healthiness and well-being.
